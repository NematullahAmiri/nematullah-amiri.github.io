About Me

I am a PMP®-certified Project Management, Public Financial Management (PFM), and Data Science professional with over 12 years of experience working across international development, government institutions, and global consulting firms including USAID, DFID, Deloitte Consulting LLP, and Adam Smith International. My career spans technical advisory roles, project leadership, and consulting assignments in complex and high-risk environments. Between 2010 and 2013, I provided technical assistance in Public Financial Management, supporting improvements in budgeting, financial systems, and institutional performance within government structures. In addition to my core expertise, I have been actively supporting organizations through remote consulting services, specializing in proposal development, business planning, and strategic documentation. I have contributed to the design of competitive proposals, funding strategies, and operational plans that enable organizations to secure opportunities and scale their impact. Today, I focus on integrating data science with project management and financial systems to deliver smarter, data-driven solutions. By applying machine learning, predictive analytics, and performance modeling, I help organizations anticipate risks, optimize resource allocation, and improve decision-making across projects and programs. What differentiates me is the ability to bridge finance, strategy, and technology—combining PFM expertise, project management discipline, and analytical capabilities to deliver measurable and sustainable results. I am currently open to opportunities in Data Science, Project Management, Public Financial Management, and Consulting roles, where I can contribute to high-impact, data-driven solutions in complex environments.
