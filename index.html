About

I am a PMP®-certified Project Management, Public Financial Management (PFM), and Data Science professional with over 14 years of experience delivering high-impact results across international development, government systems, and global consulting firms including USAID, DFID, Deloitte Consulting LLP, and Adam Smith International. My career has been built at the intersection of strategy, finance, and execution. From providing technical assistance in public financial management to leading complex multi-stakeholder projects, I have consistently helped organizations strengthen financial systems, improve performance, and deliver measurable outcomes in high-risk and resource-constrained environments. In parallel, I have expanded my expertise into data science and analytics—applying machine learning, predictive modeling, and data-driven frameworks to enhance decision-making, optimize project performance, and anticipate risks before they occur. I also provide remote consulting services in proposal development and business planning, supporting organizations to design competitive proposals, secure funding, and scale their operations. My work has contributed to the development of strategic documents, funding frameworks, and operational plans that drive sustainable growth. What differentiates me is the ability to bridge disciplines—combining public financial management, project delivery, and advanced analytics into a single, integrated approach that delivers both strategic insight and practical results. I am currently open to opportunities in Data Science, Project Management, Public Financial Management, and Consulting—particularly in complex, high-impact, and challenging environments where data-driven solutions can create real transformation.
